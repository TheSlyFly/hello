Drag Queen

The word drag means 'Dressed as a girl' and comes from a time when women were not allowed to be actresses.

  • Scientific Name: Dragonaeris Queeneeoritis
  • Average Length: 195 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 78 years
  • Habitat: Cage aux Folles

Drag Queens have been around for 100 of years, unfortunately not always celebrated as they should be. They are not necessarily gay, most live dull and ordinary lives, until at night when they come out wearing bright colors, mimicking to famous tunes. ABBAs 'Dancing Queen' is, of course, a favorite.
